EduMax

More Details

EduMax

    Edumax is an innovative internal tool designed for CDW Corporation to track employee learning progress, schedule reviews, allocate reviewers, and showcase leaderboards for every task!

    Features

  • Exercise Progress Tracking - Each participant can individually monitor their progress in every curriculum they are enrolled in!

  • Review Sheduling - Once participants have completed their learning, it's best to test their knowledge. They can click "Ready for Review," and the application will automatically assign available reviewers.

  • Realtime update on Exercise Status - Participants can view their exercise status in real-time, with updates reflecting any changes made by reviewers or admins.

  • Leaderboard for Curriculums - Healthy competition is always beneficial. To encourage participants, a leaderboard is available for every curriculum, motivating them to perform better!

  • SSO (Single Sign-On) - Integrated single sign-on from another internal product ensures data consistency and provides a hassle-free login experience for users.

    • Role && Responsibilities

  • Designed and Developed Reusable Frontend Components for EduMax Platform.

  • Integrated frontend components to backend ensure data consistency.

  • Developed reusable components that are utilized throughout the application.

  • Implemented key features including search debouncing, comprehensive exercise search capabilities, participant-filtered exercise status.

    • Tech Stack Used
    React JS, Node JS, Express JS, Mongo DB, Recoil
